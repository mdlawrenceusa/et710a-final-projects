Welcome to the Boom Beach Headquarters Commander!

Boom Beach is a base building strategy based game. Here you will have to work with your others around the world in TaskForces in order to defeat the evil Blackgruard!

One of the biggest aspects of Boom Beach is the community created and ran Hopper Network. The Hopper Network consists of thousands of online players who support each other by going around (Hopping) TaskForces and helping defeat Operations.

Operations consist of various mega bases that require the contributions of multiple players to defeat. These Operations can scale anywhere from very easy to extremely difficult. Because this is a strategy-based-game, these high level and difficult Operations require immense skills to defeat

You can Download Boom Beach in the "Download" Tab

In the "Join The Discussions" tab you will find links to group chatting apps "Telegram" and "Discord". These apps are what the thousands of Hopper Network players use to communcicate. In this tab you will also find the Official Boom Beach Twitter and Facebook pages

The "GBE Calculator" Tab contains the Gun Boat Energy Calculator which thousands of players use in order to play out and calculate their attacks on the most difficult of bases

The "Contacts" Tab contains my information as well as the information of Boom Beach
